Frequently Asked Questions

What is CartGo?

CartGo is an independent price tracking app that helps Costco members in New Zealand and Australia track their purchases and get alerted when prices drop within 30 days, so they can claim a refund at the Costco service desk.

Is CartGo affiliated with any retailer?

No. CartGo is developed by Arisdy Limited, a New Zealand software company. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any warehouse club or retailer. CartGo is an independent consumer tool.

How does price tracking work?

CartGo uses community-powered data. When users scan Costco price tags in-store, the price information is shared (anonymously) with all CartGo users. This crowdsourced approach keeps pricing data accurate and up-to-date.

Is CartGo free?

CartGo is free to download and use. Core features like scanning, searching, and the shopping cart are always free. A Pro subscription ($2.99/month) unlocks price drop alerts, watchlist, and rebate tracking. New users get a 1-month free trial of Pro.

What data does CartGo collect?

CartGo collects your email (via Google or Apple Sign-In), scanned purchase data, and approximate location (to detect which store you're near). Receipt images are processed by AI and immediately discarded. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app (Profile > Delete Account) or by emailing support@cartgo.app. All your data is permanently removed. See our account deletion page for details.
