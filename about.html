About Us

Meet the company behind CartGo

Our Company

CartGo is developed and operated by Arisdy Limited, a software development company registered in New Zealand. We specialize in creating practical mobile applications that help people save time and money in their everyday lives.

Founded with a mission to make smart shopping accessible to everyone, Arisdy Limited is committed to building tools that empower consumers through technology and community collaboration.

Our Mission

We believe that tracking prices and claiming rebates shouldn't be complicated. Our mission is to help Costco members in New Zealand and Australia save money by:

  • Providing real-time, community-powered price tracking
  • Automating the tedious work of monitoring price changes
  • Alerting members when they're eligible for rebates
  • Building a transparent and accurate price database

Why We Built CartGo

Costco's 30-day price adjustment policy is one of the best consumer benefits available, but it's often underutilized. Most shoppers simply don't have the time to track prices on everything they buy.

CartGo was created to solve this problem. By combining AI-powered receipt scanning with crowdsourced price data, we've built an app that does the tracking for you and notifies you when you're eligible for money back.
